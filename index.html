    FIFTEEN YEARS OF

    MARVEL

Marvel movies, part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), feature interconnected stories of superheroes from Marvel Comics. Key heroes include Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and Spider-Man. The MCU is divided into phases, each expanding the universe and culminating in major team-up events like The Avengers and Avengers: Endgame. Known for its action, humor, and emotional depth, the MCU includes post-credit scenes that hint at future films. It has achieved significant box office success and cultural impact.
